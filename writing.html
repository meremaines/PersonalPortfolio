WRITING FOR GOOD

Crafting copy about humanitarian development can be an agent of change—especially for your own humanity.

Read below to find out how four years at a non-profit changed me for good.

WRITING SAMPLES

For four years, I looked into the eyes of people dying from the lack of safe drinking water. And for four years, I had to hold onto hope that we really could make a difference, even in the face of systemic brokennessâ€”far outside the confines of our control.

But in the midst of what could easily become absolutely demoralizing, in remote areas of Haiti and Zimbabwe, I met everyday leaders who were inspiring their communities toward lasting change. Those memories encourage me to look for the good, even in tragedy, and to look for the commonalities we all share, no matter the divisions of mere geography.

I hope the fact that I adopted the voice of a faith-based non-profit for four years proves I can adapt to any audience without regard to ideological differences.
